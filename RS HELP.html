How to Use Review Sphere

This section explains how you can use Review Sphere to add, view, and manage reviews on movies, music, and other topics. Follow the steps below:

1. To add a review, click on the 'Add Review' button at the top.

2. Fill out the form by adding a photo, a review name, and your detailed review, then click 'Submit'.

3. You can browse other reviews by selecting categories like Movies, Music, or Art from the top menu.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: How do I edit my review?
A: You can edit your review by clicking on the edit icon next to your review on your profile page.

Q2: How do I delete a review?
A: To delete a review, go to your profile and select the delete option for the review you want to remove.

Q3: Can I contact support?
A: Yes, you can contact our support team by emailing Reviewsphere@ssru.ac.th or calling us at 0840677308.
